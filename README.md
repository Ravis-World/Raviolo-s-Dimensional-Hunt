Raviolo's Dimensional Hunt
==========================

Overview
--------

**Raviolo's Dimensional Hunt** is a branching console adventure game set in the wider _[Raviolo The Magician](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLRhal9PyMmPttbSB7YvDoUY3SHRxfJ2qJ)_ universe. After a mysterious robber steals an enormous hoard of rubies, Raviolo and his team must track the thief across multiple dimensions. Each world hides clues, dangers, and fragments of the stolen treasure.

The journey begins in a strange hub known as **the Easter Bunny's Hollow**, located on the distant planet **51 Eridani**. From this underground nexus, portals lead to various dimensions that Raviolo has encountered before. The Hollow acts as the team's base of operations while they follow the trail of the stolen rubies.

The game combines exploration, branching decisions, and tense encounters. Choices determine how situations unfold, and not every decision leads to survival.

Gameplay
--------

The game is presented as a **text-based interactive adventure** played entirely in the terminal or console window.

Players progress by reading the story and choosing numbered options when decisions appear. Many events branch into different outcomes depending on the choices made.

Features include:

*   Branching narrative paths
*   Multiple decision points
*   Success and failure outcomes
*   Character dialogue between Raviolo and his team
*   Horror elements and unexpected encounters
*   Environmental puzzles and situational challenges
    
Because the story branches frequently, different choices may reveal new scenes or lead to entirely different results.

Structure
---------

The game is divided into chapters. Each chapter represents a segment of the investigation and may contain multiple paths depending on player decisions.

Although the story can branch in many directions, all choices contribute to the larger mystery surrounding the missing ruby hoard.

The game is designed to encourage replaying sections to discover alternative outcomes.

Controls
--------

The game is played using simple keyboard input.

When a choice appears:
1.  Type the number corresponding to your chosen option.
2.  Press **Enter** to confirm.
    
The game will also occasionally pause and wait for the player to press **Enter** to continue reading.
